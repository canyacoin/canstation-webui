What is Gas?

Behind the scenes

When someone talks about “gas” they are usually referring to either

  • Gas limit
  • Gas price

Gas limit is the maximum amount of gas you are willing to spend on a transaction. Generally, the recommended gas limit you should set in your transaction are:

  • 21,000 for standard transactions.
  • 100,000 for sending tokens (ERC20 Tokens)
  • 500,000 for executing contracts.

Gas price is usually measured in GWei, and you can set this price to determine how much you are willing to spend per unit of gas. CanStation allows you to choose the GWei to help you in turn estimate the speed that your transaction will be mined, taking into account of the current network conditions.

Transaction Fees (Tx Fees) are calculated by Gas Limit * Gas Price

Be smart about the numbers you enter for “Gas Limit” and “Gwei” when you send or sign your transaction!
